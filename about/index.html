About

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the guidance of Prof.Charles Zhang. Before that, I received my bachelor’s degree in computer science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. During my undergraduate study, I worked closely with Biaoshuai Tao, Hongfei Fu, Qinxiang Cao and Yuhao Zhang. Thanks for these professors’ patient guidance.

Teaching Assistant

Research Interests

My research interests include program static analysis and theoretical computer science problems. More recenltly, I have been studying problems in:

  • GPGPU
